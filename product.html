ABOUT BRAND

Tailored with love and passion for nature, Rainy Days jackets are the best adventurer’s choice. Founded in the mid-‘80s, we have learned with time how to create the best fit for nature lovers. As a result, our products can guarantee you the comfort and the confidence that your next adventure will be memorable.

Our picks for the best rain and winter jackets are among the warmest on the market. The jackets are packed with down fill and built to stand up to freezing temperatures and howling winds.
