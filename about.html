Story

Tailored with love and passion for nature, Rainy Days jackets are the best adventurer’s choice. Opened in the mid-‘80s, we have learned with time how to create the best fit for nature lovers. As a result, our products can guarantee you the comfort and the confidence that your next adventure will be memorable.

Vision

Changing the way we look at nature was always our goal. Taking care of mother nature and each other is the best feeling a person can experience. Our vision is to offer quality and comfort to those who can appreciate unforgettable moments in life.
